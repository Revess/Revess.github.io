As composer and programmer, I make both worlds connect

A student at the University of Arts Utrecht, HKU for short, is a well known name in the industry. Active as a composer and as a programmer, where on occasion both fields work collide with eachother to create something beautiful.

Composing for the media and autonomously. My autonomous compositions can be defined as Intelligent Dance Music (IDM). Where I try to break free from the masses and find a niece that is still very recognizable and enjoyable for most people.
When composing for media my specialty is the ability to combine Sound-Design, orchestral and synthetic sounds.

As a programmer I have experience in multiple environments. The languages I master are C++, Javascript, web development; Max, Msp and Jitter; embedded computing. As a creative system designer I don't just write the code, I know how to communicate with a client en transform their idea into a full product, knowing what is possible with the current technology.

Both fields meet in the middle where I am able to use my skills as a composer to create software for composers. Using software to create tools that enhance the composition process freeing up time for the composer to focus on the important aspects.
